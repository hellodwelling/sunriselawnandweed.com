Our Services

What we are best at

Lawn Transformations

If you are looking to change the look and feel of your lawn, we can help you dream what is possible and bring it to life. We specialize in growing grass. We can also offer advice on how you can make your lawn the best it can be.

Fire Ant / Outdoor Insect Pest Control

Nothing ruins a beautiful day enjoying your lawn like fire ant bites. You no longer have to worry. We use professional strength products which eliminate fire ants and other unwanted outdoor pests while safe around people and pets. We observe best practices when applying insecticides so that we do not harm the honey bees.

Weed Control

We will get rid of the unwanted weeds in your lawn. Weed control is key to overall health of your lawn and important to not overlook for long term health. Improves overall curb appeal and helps your turf stay thick and beautiful.

Mowing

A big part of lawn maintenance is ensuring your lawn is regularly mowed. We know life gets busy, and we are here to help keep your lawn looking fresh.

Lawn Aeration

A healthy lawn starts with proper aeration to ensure that the proper nutrients and air are reaching the roots of your grass. We have the equipment and experience necessary.

Fertilization

Fertilization will help your lawn by improving overall health and making it greener and thicker. Fertilization helps the lawn not only look better, but last longer.

Mulch and Pine Needle Installation

Mulch is used to help maintain the health of your landscape and adds a professional touch to any planter. It can be placed over soil to lock in moisture and improve soil conditions to encourage a healthier lawn. We provide a variety of professional mulching services.

Overseeding

This is the planting of grass seed directly into existing turf, without tearing up the turf, or the soil. It is a way to fill in bare spots, improve the density of the grass, and enhance your lawn's color.

Hedge Trimming

Hedge trimming can transform your property. Our experienced landscapers will rid your home or business of overgrowth and keep your shrubs looking immaculate. We handle seasonal services for fall and spring clean up.

Soil Samples

A big part of turf health is knowing exactly what nutrients are needed. We use soil samples to evaluate your current soil health and we create a specialized plan for your lawn. This helps not only make your lawn the very best it can be but also helps the environment by not over applying fertilizers that are not needed.

Seasonal Clean Ups

We offer seasonal cleanups especially in the spring. This can sometime be overwhelming to the homeowner. We are here to help you spruce up your property for the season.
